CinderFox

This launcher opens Firefox with a home surface and chrome styling tuned to feel like a native part of AcreetionOS instead of a generic browser tab.

Dark blue, signal blue, and warm orange accents keep the browser aligned with the rest of the desktop.

Browser Firefox profile themed with userChrome and userContent
Surface Dark blue glass with warm orange accents
Mode Dedicated wrapper, not a Firefox rebuild
